Winners & Finalists

JASMINE - SASPD - 1st Place!

Jasmine Liu from SAS PUDONG has shown and given the judging panel a strong and effective solution to the prompt of Covid-19. She has put in a lot of work for her idea's which can be seen in her presentation which includes accurate cost calculations to intricate explanations of her own visual scanning and recognition code, which have been explained clearly in a well thought out and articulated presentation. Thus, she has been awarded the grand prize of the Tech Mahindra internship this year. Watch her presentation here, explore her github code and her 3D model for this Congrats Jasmine Liu!
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Michael Du - SASPD - 2nd Place!

With a very creative and out of the box solution which initially confused the judging panel with the concept of using a musical instrument, the Reform Bassoon. Michael Du gets awarded the second place in this year's tech tournament, with his well crafted, interesting and surprising take on this prompt which he managed to justify his solution and idea with his intricate presentation, which included all the necessary details such as production costs to scaleability, led for the judging panel to award him second place for this year's tech tournament. Great job Michael! Explore his < href="https://youtu.be/arYvatP8ivI" class="text-success" target="_blank">Presentation, and his 3D model on tinkercad
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Neev Jain - 3rd Place

With a strong and confident presentation with relevant details, a creative idea and a interesting solution with supporting data for his claims and designing a fully autonomous vehicle for the covid-19 situation while keeping factors of economics and business in mind has led neev to be the recipient of the third place for this year's Innovator's Tournament.
