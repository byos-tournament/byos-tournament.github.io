ABOUT BYOS

BYOS DIGITAL SSIS

We were the first team in shanghai to create a full virtual campus of the our own school, this model is fully animated and is even a standalone application, every window, every screw and every strand of grass was modeled by hand. this massive project took over a year to develop with just a team of 6 people. Watch the trailer for this on our  > WeChat Channels <

BYOS VR ILC

BYOS also created a full building of our school in Virtual Reality, all doors, windows and objects are intractable, this model is accurate to the core, even the screws in a door frame are in the VR model, this all was also 100% to scale to not loose any accuracy and compatible with all of the world's leading VR headsets. Watch the trailer for the VR ILC > WeChat Channels <

BYOS LAUNCH EVENT

A live Event with over 200 audience members that BYOS carried out, sharing and presenting the two products we developed and our journey as BYOS. Watch post-even interviews on our WeChat channels!
