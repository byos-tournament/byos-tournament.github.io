Tournament Information!

Please make sure to read every section on this page to ensure the accurate understanding of the rules and requirements of this competition before registering and choosing to participate!

Eligibility Criteria                                 

- Open to all Shanghai high school students in Grade 9 - 12
- All work must be done and submitted in English
- Students must be located within Shanghai
- Must be in Shanghai during July and/or August of 2022, as internship office is in Pudong.

Content Requirements                                     

Include/discuss the following aspects about your idea:

1. Impact: How significantly will this product touch the lives of people during COVID-19?

2. Creativity: How unique and original is your idea?

3. Cost effectiveness: Is your solution too expensive? Explain how you have priced your item, and the raw materials that have led to that pricing.

4. Feasibility: What is the science behind your idea and how achievable is it? Could cities around the world realistically implement it?

5. Scalability: Can your product be made available to the masses? What strategies will you use to spread your product across the market?

6. Visualization: How does your product look like? Help us visualize and see the product through sketches, illustrations, or even better - a 3D model of the product easily made on SketchUp/tinkercad,etc… (Candidates who submit a 3D model file will be awarded extra points) 

Submission format

- Submissions can be in any form you think most effectively presents your idea.
- PowerPoint/Keynote (recommended)
- Video (recommended)
- Prezi
- Word document
- What’s important is that your format allows you to explain and present the 6 Content Requirements with sufficient detail.
- Note: For candidates who would like their ideas to be easily shared, adding a voice overlay onto your presentation, and submitting in mp4 format is highly advised. This will allow for your submission to be shared on BYOS social media platforms.

Info for WINNERS!

On the 20th of June, we will release the names of the 3 tournament winners.

- The winners will be contacted both via email and WeChat.

- The #1 candidate will receive a free and exclusive summer internship with Tech Mahindra. The candidate will be sent all contacts of Tech Mahindra staff, and office address. The internship is schedule to commence on July, and end in August, however the exact dates and timings can be worked out by the Tech Mahindra and the candidate himself!

- Internship aside, the #1, #2, and #3 winners will each be individually invited to an episode of The BYOS Podcast (our official technology podcast), to go in depth with their product ideas. Our podcast is totally informal and conversational, so we’ll grab a snack and have a fun discussion.
